Frequently Asked Questions

What size PNG should I use for favicon conversion?
Use a square PNG image at least 512x512 pixels. The converter will generate all smaller sizes automatically while maintaining quality.
Will my PNG transparency be preserved in the favicon?
Yes, PNG transparency is fully preserved in the generated favicons. Your icon will display cleanly on any browser tab background.
What favicon files are generated from my PNG?
You get favicon.ico (32x32), favicon-16x16.png, favicon-32x32.png, apple-touch-icon.png (180x180), android-chrome-192x192.png, and android-chrome-512x512.png.
Can I convert a PNG with a white background?
Yes, but for best results use a PNG with a transparent background. White backgrounds may not look good on dark browser themes.
Is this PNG to favicon converter free?
Yes, completely free with no registration required. Your images are processed in your browser and never uploaded to our servers.
